About

SG Dividends Reports is an independent personal project — a convenience layer over publicly available SGX corporate-announcement data for a selected set of Mainboard-listed companies. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Singapore Exchange Limited (SGX) or any of the companies listed on this site. There is no AI, no scoring, and no recommendation — only a calendar plus links to the underlying SGX or company pages.

Data sources

  • SGX website — primary source for announcements and corporate actions.
  • Yahoo Finance website — primary source for stock price histories.
  • Company investor-relations pages — fallback for report PDFs.
