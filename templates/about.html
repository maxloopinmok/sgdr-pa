About

SG Dividends Reports is a convenience layer over public SGX corporate event data for Mainboard-listed companies. There is no AI, no scoring, and no recommendation — only a calendar plus links to the underlying SGX or company pages.

Data sources

  • SGX website — primary source for announcements and corporate actions.
  • Company investor-relations pages — fallback for report PDFs.
